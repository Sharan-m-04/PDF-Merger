NOTE: When using our PDF merger website, please ensure that the PDF files you wish to merge are named in ascending order. This means that the file names should be numbered or alphabetized in sequence, such as "1.pdf, 2.pdf, 3.pdf..." or "a.pdf, b.pdf, c.pdf...". By following this naming convention, our website can easily and accurately merge your files in the correct order, ensuring that your final document is organized and easy to navigate. If your files are not named in ascending order, there is a risk that the merged document may be disorganized and difficult to use. Therefore, we strongly recommend that you take the time to rename your files before merging them on our website.

How To Use

Step-1: Choose the PDF files that you want to merge. You can either drag and drop the files onto the website or click the "Choose" button to browse and select the files from your computer.

Step-2: Arrange the files in the correct order if necessary. If the files are not already named in ascending order.

Step-3: Click on the "Merge" button to start the merging process and the file will download automatically after the process.

Step-4: After downloading or saving the merged PDF file, check to make sure that all pages and content are in the correct order and that there are no errors or missing pages.

That's it! With these simple steps, you can quickly and easily merge your PDF files into one organized document.
